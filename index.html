BEGINNER GUIDE

MASTER THE EMOJIS

🐉 Dragon Emoji

The Dragon destroys every emoji currently on the screen. Use it when the arena becomes overloaded.

💣 Bomb Emoji

The Bomb is deadly. Slicing it removes one life instantly.

👹 Oni Emoji

The Oni also removes one life if sliced. Avoid it at all costs.

🚗 Car Emoji

The Car splits into more emojis when sliced. Slice all of them for bonus points.

🥑 Avocado Emoji

The Avocado duplicates into more targets. Clear them quickly to maximize score.

🤡 Clown Emoji

The Clown multiplies after slicing. Keep slicing every clone for extra points.

👼 Baby Angel Emoji

Restores one life when sliced. Never miss it.

🪽 Wing Emoji

Wings also restore your life. Slice them fast before they disappear.

⚡ Lightning Emoji

The Lightning emoji zaps through the arena with electric chaos.

🧊 Ice Cube Emoji

The Ice Cube freezes the screen temporarily, giving you more time to slice extra emojis safely.
